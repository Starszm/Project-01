Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is rich, moist, and perfect for any occasion. It is easy to make and tastes delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, then mix well.
  4. Pour into a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
