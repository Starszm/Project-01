Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s. It follows a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana, as they uncover supernatural mysteries involving secret government experiments and a parallel dimension called the Upside Down.

Visit Official Website

Cast & Creative Team

Created by the Duffer Brothers. Main cast includes: Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Gaten Matarazzo, and Caleb McLaughlin.

Characters

Eleven – A girl with telekinetic powers.
Mike Wheeler – Leader of the friend group.
Dustin Henderson – The smart and funny one.
Jim Hopper – The town’s police chief.

Reviews

The show has been praised for its nostalgic 80s atmosphere, strong storytelling, and character development. It holds high ratings on major review platforms.
